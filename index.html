The man behind the name

A life shaped by grief, leadership, fatherhood, and imperfect faith.

I was born in Zimbabwe and now live in Cape Town. I have led teams under pressure, buried my mother, carried responsibility across borders, and learned how quietly heavy a life can become while still looking capable from the outside.

I do not write as a polished authority. I write as a fellow traveler who knows what it is to keep going while still trying to understand what God is doing in the dark.

MEN Book in progress

In the works

A book about biblical manhood, written in the margins of a full life.

For three years I have been writing a book for men who are trying to become steady without pretending they have everything figured out. It is about responsibility, formation, failure, recovery, and the kind of strength that serves instead of performs.

It is not here to impress anyone. It is here to accompany men who are carrying more than they say.
